Everyone is entitled to their own opinion. As, am I... so here we go... TomatoCart (albeit a purely absurd name) has an beautiful design from the inside out. The admin area is nearly it's own webOS... what other cart do you know that has even THOUGHT about this? We've tried osCommerce, PrestaShop, CRE Loaded and before now OpenCart... all had their unsurmountable flaws (mostly front end visible and technical challenges) which surfaced within 48-72 hours with working with them. However, TomatoCart... out of the box... even with demo data... simply "wow'd" us and practically begged to show us more.

This review isn't going without noticing a few hiccups.... albeit them quite minimal especially for a package of this dev and aesthetic sophistication... adding currency has stumped us this evening.... as well as some of the backend PayPal API information that hasn't hasn't been required in previous carts. That said, I'm fully on board not to mention ready and willing to grow alongside of TomatoCart! (On sideline note.... dudes... change your name... you guys were a last ditch attempt for us strictly for your name... sounds incredibly remedial and unsophisticated... and for a robust eCommerce SOLUTION as you truly are, your software name is exceedingly unattractive and does zero justice to what you guys have accomplished. Again, my two cents worth!)

Cheers!
